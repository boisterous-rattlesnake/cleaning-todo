# ✨ SparklePoints: Daily Cleaning Tracker

SparklePoints is a lightweight, mobile-friendly web application designed to gamify your daily cleaning routine. Earn points for every task you complete and track your lifetime progress.

## 🚀 Features
* **Daily Points System**: Earn 1 point for every cleaning task checked off.
* **The 10-Point Cap**: Encourages consistency without burnout—you can earn a maximum of 10 points per day.
* **Lifetime Tracker**: Watch your total points grow over time.
* **Persistence**: Uses `localStorage` to save your tasks and points directly in your browser. No login required!
* **Automatic Reset**: Daily points reset at midnight, while your uncompleted tasks carry over to the next day.

## 📱 How to Use (iPhone/Android)
1. Open the [Live Link](YOUR_GITHUB_PAGES_URL_HERE) in Safari or Chrome.
2. Tap the **Share** icon (iOS) or **Menu** icon (Android).
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen."**
4. The app will now appear on your home screen and function like a native application.

## 🛠️ Built With
* **HTML5 & CSS3** (Tailwind CSS for modern styling)
* **Vanilla JavaScript** (Logic and data persistence)
* **Local Storage API** (To keep your data private and local to your device)

## ⚠️ Important Note
Since this app stores data in your browser's local cache:
* Clearing your browser's "Site Data" or "Cookies" will reset your points and tasks.
* Data is specific to the device and browser you are currently using; it does not sync across multiple devices.
